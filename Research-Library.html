About Timeless Compass Research Library

Curated by the Sri Sri Institute for Advanced Research (SSIAR), this research library explores 248 published studies on ancient systems of knowledge — connecting scientific inquiry with wisdom traditions.

Exploration Flow

📊 Macro View — Strategic overview: scorecards, charts, map & trends.
📋 Meso View — Browse & compare studies in the database.
🔬 Micro View — Preview a selected study before accessing the original publication.

How to use this Research Library

🔍 Search by keyword, author, title or journal.

🔎 Advanced Search to filter by topic, population, intervention & more.

📊 Click any chart element to instantly filter the database.

📋 Click a database row to preview study details on the right.

✕ Active filter chips show what's applied — click × to remove.

🔗 Link to Published Study opens the original source.
